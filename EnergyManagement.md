# EnergyManagement

## Manage Energy not Time
### Activities that make me relax
- Eating Favorite Food
- Listening to music
- Watching movies
- Taking a walk
- Deep breathing
- Spending quiet time alone

### When I get into the Stress quadrant
- Learning something new
- Tight deadlines
- Solving difficult problems
- Exams or interviews
- Heavy workload

### How I know I am in the Excitement quadrant
- Feeling motivated and curious
- Losing track of time while working
- Feeling energetic and positive
- Enjoying the challenge
- Wanting to do more

## Sleep is your superpower
### Sleep is your Superpower (brief points)
- Sleep improves learning and memory
- Sleep boosts immunity
- Sleep regulates emotions
- Sleep improves focus and productivity
- Lack of sleep harms physical and mental health

### Ideas to sleep better
- Fixed sleep and wake-up time
- Avoid screens before bed
- No caffeine at night
- Quiet and dark room
- Light dinner

## Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise
### Benefits of Exercise
- Improves memory
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Boosts mood
- Increases focus
- Slows brain aging

### Steps to exercise more
- Daily walking
- Short workouts
- Morning stretching
- Use stairs instead of lift
- Set a fixed exercise time
